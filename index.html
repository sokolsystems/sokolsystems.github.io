Our mission

Equipped with decades of FinTech experience and inspired by tremendous changes in the software industry, Sokol Systems brings you the fastest Asset Record System on the market. The system is capable of satisfying your most challenging requirements in performance, functionality, reliability and cost.

 

User Benefits

  • Ultra-high performance with throughput of 80,000 business transactions per second
  • Rich set of functional and non-funtional requirements covering all aspects of the Asset Record operations and maintenance
  • The entire Asset Record system, and the data it operates on, runs on a single commodity computer

 

Features

  • Double Entry Integrity of T-accounts
  • Flexible Journalling Rules
  • Broad Support for Financial Instruments and Standard Position Buckets
  • Projected, Contractual and Actual Position Settlement Types
  • As-Of and As-At Processing
  • Super Fast On-demand Snapshots
  • Duplicate Detection
  • Multiple Markets and Multiple Currencies
  • Support for Client, Broker and Firm Positions
  • Automated and Manual End-of-Day
  • Efficient Recovery Options
  • Flexible Storage Options
